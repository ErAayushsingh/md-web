Privacy Policy

At Machadalo, your privacy is a priority. This policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and handle your Personally Identifiable Information (PII) in line with applicable privacy laws, including those in the United States and India.

Please read this document carefully to understand how your information is handled on our website.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you visit our website, register, or place an order, you may be asked to provide personal details such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number

Non-Personal Information

We may also collect non-personal data such as:

  • Browser type and version
  • IP address
  • Geolocation
  • Date and time of your visit

2. When We Collect Information

We collect information when you:

  • Register on our website
  • Make a purchase
  • Subscribe to our newsletter
  • Fill out a form

3. How We Use Your Information

The information collected from you is used for:

  • Personalizing your experience and delivering tailored content
  • Improving customer service and responding to inquiries
  • Sending periodic emails regarding your orders or services
  • Enhancing website performance and user experience

4. How We Protect Your Information

We prioritize the security of your personal data by employing:

  • Regular malware scanning
  • Secure data storage practices

While we do not currently use SSL certificates, we ensure that sensitive information is never requested, such as credit card details.

5. Use of Cookies

Cookies are small files stored on your computer to enhance user experience. We use cookies to:

  • Remember user preferences
  • Compile site traffic and interaction data

You can disable cookies through your browser settings. However, some website features may not function properly.

6. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your PII to outside parties without notice. Trusted third parties who assist in website operation or business services must agree to keep your information confidential.

We may share non-personal, aggregated data for marketing purposes.

7. Fair Information Practices

In the event of a data breach, we will notify users via email within 7 business days. We adhere to the Individual Redress Principle, allowing individuals to pursue legal recourse for data misuse.
